Tired of Outdated Systems?

Prone to Errors

Manual roll calls and entries are slow, inefficient, and frequently lead to inaccuracies and manipulation.

Security Risks

Card-tapping systems are vulnerable to misuse, like buddy punching, compromising campus and workplace security.

Hygiene Concerns

Traditional biometric systems require physical contact, posing significant hygiene risks, especially post-pandemic.

The BEAT Advantage

Dual Verification

Combines long-range RFID and AI Facial Recognition to ensure authenticated presence, eliminating fraud.

Hands-Free Automation

Individuals are logged automatically as they walk past. No tapping, no pausing, no disruption.

Live Alerts & Reports

Receive instant SMS/email notifications for mismatches and access real-time data on a central dashboard.

Edge + Cloud Architecture

Real-time processing on the edge with secure data sync to the cloud for historical records and analytics.

Simple, Seamless, Secure

1. Approach

A student or employee walks towards the entry point.

2. Detect

The BEAT device detects the RFID tag and captures a facial image from up to 5 meters.

3. Verify

The system cross-references both inputs for a successful dual authentication match.

4. Log

Attendance is logged in real-time, and data is synced securely to the cloud dashboard.
